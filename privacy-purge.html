Privacy Policy

Purge Cleaner (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use the Purge Cleaner mobile application (the “App”).

Purge Cleaner is a utility application designed to help users organize device storage, identify unnecessary files, and optimize device space through tools such as duplicate detection, media organization, and contact cleanup.

This Privacy Policy applies to users located in the European Union, United States, Japan, and other regions where the application may be available.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Overview of the Application

Purge Cleaner is designed to help users maintain organized device storage by identifying items that may be unnecessary or duplicated.

The App may provide features including but not limited to:

  • detection of duplicate photos and videos
  • identification of similar images
  • analysis of large media files
  • contact organization tools
  • detection of duplicate or incomplete contacts
  • calendar event overview
  • battery health insights
  • operating system version reminders
  • email breach monitoring tools
  • ad-blocking assistance
  • device storage optimization suggestions

The App does not automatically delete user data without user confirmation. Users remain fully in control of any actions taken.

Information We May Access

Depending on the features used, the App may request permission to access certain information stored on your device.

Access to this information is always subject to user permission through the device operating system.

The types of information that may be accessed include:

  • media library (photos and videos)
  • contacts
  • calendar events
  • device system information

This information is used solely to provide the functionality of the App.

Media Library Access

If permission is granted, Purge Cleaner may access the user's photo and video library.

This access allows the App to help identify:

  • duplicate photos
  • similar images
  • screenshots
  • large videos
  • media that may occupy significant storage space

The purpose of this feature is to help users manage and organize their media files.

Media analysis is designed to occur locally on the user's device whenever possible.

The App does not permanently remove photos or videos without explicit user confirmation.

Contacts Access

If the user grants permission, the App may access contact information stored on the device.

This allows the App to identify potential issues such as:

  • duplicate contacts
  • incomplete contact entries
  • contacts missing names or numbers

These tools help users keep their contact list organized.

Contact information remains stored on the device and is not transferred externally except as required for the App to function.

Calendar Access

With user permission, the App may access calendar data stored on the device.

This allows the App to help users review:

  • duplicate calendar entries
  • outdated events
  • incomplete event information

The App does not modify or remove calendar events without user action.

Device Information

The App may collect certain technical information about the device in order to ensure proper operation and improve performance.

This may include:

  • device model
  • operating system version
  • application version
  • device language
  • system configuration information
  • diagnostic information

This information helps maintain compatibility and improve user experience.

Battery Health Insights

The App may display information related to device battery status using system information available on the device.

This feature may provide general insights such as:

  • battery condition indicators
  • charging patterns
  • device energy usage suggestions

This information is derived from system data and does not identify individual users.

Operating System Version Monitoring

The App may check the version of the operating system installed on the device.

This allows the App to provide suggestions regarding:

  • system updates
  • device compatibility
  • security improvements

This information is used solely to provide recommendations within the App.

Email Leakage Monitoring

Some versions of the App may allow users to check whether an email address has appeared in publicly known data breaches.

If the user enters an email address for this feature, the App may compare the address against publicly available breach databases.

This feature is optional.

The email address is used solely for the purpose of the breach check.

Ad-Blocking Assistance

The App may provide tools designed to reduce the visibility of advertisements during browsing.

Such features may include configuration guidance or network filtering tools.

The App itself does not track browsing history or collect information about the websites visited by users.

Free Trial and Subscriptions

Purge Cleaner may offer a 3-day free trial that allows users to test premium features.

Premium features may include enhanced storage analysis and extended cleanup tools.

If a subscription is not canceled before the end of the trial period, the subscription may automatically renew according to the terms displayed in the app store.

All subscription billing is handled by the platform through which the App was downloaded, such as:

  • Apple App Store

The App does not collect or store payment card information.

How Information Is Used

Information accessed by the App may be used for the following purposes:

  • providing core application features
  • identifying duplicate or unnecessary files
  • improving app performance
  • maintaining security and reliability
  • responding to user inquiries

Information is processed only as necessary to support the functionality of the App.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for certain operational purposes such as:

  • analytics
  • crash reporting
  • infrastructure services

These services may collect limited technical information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Examples of such services may include analytics or diagnostic tools commonly used in mobile applications.

Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality and maintain service quality.

In many cases, information analyzed by the App remains stored only on the user’s device.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Security practices may include:

  • encryption technologies
  • secure communication protocols
  • internal access controls

While we strive to protect user information, no system can guarantee absolute security.

International Data Transfers

Where required, appropriate safeguards may be implemented to protect personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
